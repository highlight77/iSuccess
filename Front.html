OVERVIEW
iAssist’s goal is to aid disabled individuals in finding easily-accessible locations near them. Users are able to determine whether a particular public space has available ramps, designated seating, and specified parking spaces to guarantee a friendly and smooth visit. Such locations are determined through user-reviews and feedback. Our Goals are:

Review and
Go
Provides user-reviews for visitors on various accommodations to improve experience.

Ease of
Access
Accesible via a website and a mobile app, iAssist can be used on the go.
Assurance and
Reliability
Provides up-to-date information and shows reviews from verified users.
